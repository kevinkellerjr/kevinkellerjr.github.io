This game is a group project that is being created now in my Senior year of college.

This is the Curse Area Of Effect Spell. This is an Area Of Effect Spell that can be cast at any time, but will only work when the enemy walks through it. When you cast it the effect it has on the enemy is that it will slow them down, reduce the enemy attack damage resistance, reduce the damage of the magic attacks resistance, and it will reduce the enemy attack damage.

This is the Curse Blade Spell. This is a Sword Buff Spell that can be cast at any time, but will only work when the player hits the enemy. When you cast it the effect it has on the enemy is that it will slow them down, reduce the enemy attack damage resistance, reduce the damage of the magic attacks resistance, and it will reduce the enemy attack damage.

This is the Curse Hex Spell. This is a Projectile Spell that can be cast at any time, but will only work when the projectile hits the enemy. When you cast it the effect it has on the enemy is that it will slow them down, reduce the enemy attack damage resistance, reduce the damage of the magic attacks resistance, and it will reduce the enemy attack damage.
