This game was a group project that was created my Junior year of college.

I created this gladiator system that has the list of gladiators the player can use. The gladiator system consists of the gladiator list and the stats of each gladiator.

You are able to click on each gladiator image and it will show their stats, inventory, and global inventory.

When you click on any of the gladiator's images it will update the scroll to show that gladiator's stats.
